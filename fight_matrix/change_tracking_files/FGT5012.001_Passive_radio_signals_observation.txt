================================================================================
FGT5012.001: Passive radio signals observation
Architecture Segment: 
----------------------------------------
Procedure Examples
----------------------------------------
Externals geolocation: Adversary geolocates unknown UE using some combination of Direction of Arrival, Time-of-Arrival, and/or Frequency-of-Arrival of UE signal externals.
----------------------------------------
Mitigations
----------------------------------------
FGM5099: Move UE closer to base station and/or reduce height of UE above terrain and/or move indoors or into multipath environment.
FGM5098: Reduce usage. Use UE only when needed. Turn UE off if not needed for period of time.
----------------------------------------
Pre-Conditions
----------------------------------------
Line-of-sight: Attacker must have radio line-of-sight to target for most accurate geolocation.
----------------------------------------
Critical Assets
----------------------------------------
UE location: UE/subscriber geographical location
----------------------------------------
Detection
----------------------------------------
----------------------------------------
Post-Conditions
----------------------------------------
Subsequent attack: Geolocation can make electronic attack for degradation of service more effective, bidding down to defeat ID and traffic confidentiality more effective, and can expose subscriber to physical attack.
