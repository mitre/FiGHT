================================================================================
T1464: UE or gNB Jamming
Architecture Segment:  RAN
----------------------------------------
Procedure Examples
----------------------------------------
Noise jamming via jammer device: Jammer device is used to perform noise jamming of the radio interface, See [1]
False synchronization signals: Fake UE sends false synchronization signals to the gNB
Replay legitimate signals: Fake base station replays modified portions of legitimate signals from gNB or towards gNB
----------------------------------------
Mitigations
----------------------------------------
FGM5099: Move user equipment closer to base station to overpower jamming signal.
FGM5100: Increase height of User Equipment to avoid jamming signal
FGM5001: Disable jamming source
----------------------------------------
Pre-Conditions
----------------------------------------
----------------------------------------
Critical Assets
----------------------------------------
Radio receivers at base station and user equipment: UE and gNB basic operations
----------------------------------------
Detection
----------------------------------------
FGDS5001: Identify source and location of jammer
