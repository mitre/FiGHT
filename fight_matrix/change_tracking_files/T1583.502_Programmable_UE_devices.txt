================================================================================
T1583.502: Programmable UE devices
Architecture Segment:  UE
----------------------------------------
Procedure Examples
----------------------------------------
Fake UE build: Adversary uses Software-Defined-Radio (SDR) running OAI-5G (“OpenAirInterface”) modified software
Fake UE acquisition: In [1], it is mentioned that the adversary must “forge a malicious UE”, which is then used to replay messages form the malicious (adversary controlled) gNB to the legitimate gNB
----------------------------------------
Mitigations
----------------------------------------
UE assisted false gNB detection: Legitimate UEs send measurement reports to network periodically. Network determines anomalies in those measurement reports for presence of fake base stations. See [2].
