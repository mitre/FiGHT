FGT5025: Falsify interconnect invoice
Architecture Segment: 
----------------------------------------
Procedure Examples
----------------------------------------
False charging: Signaling fraud may be undertaken by a partner operator, via false charging over international signaling interconnection. In [1] section 5.3. Reference [2] mentions service fraud.
----------------------------------------
Mitigations
----------------------------------------
FGM5503: Employ home-routing instead of local breakout for user traffic (but this means more delay and lower quality of service)
