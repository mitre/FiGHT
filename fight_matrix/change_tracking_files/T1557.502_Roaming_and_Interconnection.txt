================================================================================
T1557.502: Roaming and Interconnection
Architecture Segment:   Roaming
----------------------------------------
Procedure Examples
----------------------------------------
----------------------------------------
Mitigations
----------------------------------------
M1037: Ensure only traffic from expected sources can reach the SEPP
M1035: Minimize access to vSEPP from limited locations such as a privileged access workstation
M1030: Limit network exposure of vSEPP from other core services in hPLMN
----------------------------------------
Pre-Conditions
----------------------------------------
SEPP control: Adversary would need to be in control of the vSEPP which may be managed by a VAS
----------------------------------------
Critical Assets
----------------------------------------
SEPP function: Adversary would target the SEPP
----------------------------------------
Detection
----------------------------------------
DS0015: Monitor for access to SEPP application/appliance for unexpected access.
DS0029: Analyze network traffic to/from SEPP to determine if from unexpected source/dest and consistent with expected traffic from other operators.
