================================================================================
T1195.501: SIM credential theft
Architecture Segment: 
----------------------------------------
Procedure Examples
----------------------------------------
Infiltrate SIM vendor’s network.: Allegedly: the UK agency GCHQ planted malware on Gemalto servers to obtain SIM credentials. The number of keys obtained were in the order of millions. [1]
----------------------------------------
Mitigations
----------------------------------------
M1017: Personnel security: Train personnel in SIM card OEMs to be wary of social engineering and other attempts of unauthorized parties to gain access to any relevant resource, and to report suspicious activities
M1030: Physical and cyber security of IT systems, servers.
M1022: Restrict access to files exchanged between SIM vendor and MNO
M1041: Protect the files where SIM data is stored by encryption
FGM1557: Protect the files where SIM data is stored by integrity protection

----------------------------------------
----------------------------------------
Critical Assets
----------------------------------------
Privacy of subscriber data and voice traffic: Adversary is after getting the keys to decrypt cellular communications for those sets of SIMs whose credentials it captured.
